Interface of any online store plays a huge role in drawing the attention of people which eventually turn the visitors into potential buyers. A good interface increases the reachability to the products; hence, it is important to look for the ways to enhance the interface of any website.

Mega menus in the websites can help you to enhance the visibility of each product available in your online store. Mega menu is a two-dimensional drop-down website menu interface visible on the Odoo website to increase reachability of the products. All the options available are shown under a specified category which contains a group of related products.

Now, you can hassle-freely manage mega menus on your Odoo website too. Odoo Website Mega Menu facilitates you to add a mega menu to your Odoo Website so that you can add products to it and link them to their respective product page.

Configure the mega menu in Odoo Website as you Want

There are multiple templates for mega menus. You can set them from the styling menu in advance options while creating a mega menu. You can also add the background images and icons in the mega menu as per your requirements.

These mega menus are elegantly designed to draw the attention of the customers and easily land to the right product page as per their requirements.

There are multiple styling options available for the mega menu so that you can make it as enticing as you want without any hassle.
